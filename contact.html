About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to leverage IoT technology to enhance the accuracy and efficiency of volleyball boundary detection. We strive to provide real-time classification of ball interactions and human movements on the court, enabling coaches, players, and officials to make data-driven decisions that elevate performance and gameplay experience.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leading innovator in sports analytics through our IoT-based solution. We envision a future where our technology not only transforms volleyball training and competitions but also sets new standards for precision and reliability in sports event management. By continuously advancing our capabilities, we aim to empower stakeholders across the volleyball community with actionable insights that drive excellence and competitiveness.
